Maintenance Scheduling Optimisation via Discrete-Event Simulation

🏭

Simulation-based evaluation of three maintenance strategies (Breakdown, Preventive, Predictive) for a 24/7 sulfuric acid plant using Rockwell Arena and OptQuest. TOPSIS multi-criteria ranking across 18 scenarios achieved a 96.6% reduction in annual downtime and 91.5% cut in monthly maintenance cost.

Arena Simulation Discrete-Event Simulation OptQuest TOPSIS Predictive Maintenance MSc Thesis
